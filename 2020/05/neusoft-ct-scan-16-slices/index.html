CT Scan 16 Slices Neusoft

Neusoft CT Scan 16 Slices Specifications

Gantry

Focal Spot to Detector Distance: Not more than 1050mm

  • Aperture: At least 70cm
  • Rotation Speed/360° : 0.78 second or less

Laser Light for Patient Positioning: 5 or more

  • Tilt Angle : At least ± 30° mechanical

Table

  • Load Capacity: At least 205kg
  • Min Table Feed Speed: 1mm/s or less
  • Max Table Feed Speed: 100mm/s or more
  • Scannable Range: 1550mm or more
  • Couch vertical movement Range: 430mm(or less) -970mm(or higher)

Tube

  • Focal Spot Size: 0.4mm x 0.7mm or smaller, 0.6mm ×1.3mm or larger
  • Tube Size: At least 4MHU

Tube Cooling Rate : 667 KHU/min or higher

Generator

  • Type: High Frequency compact generator
  • Max Power: At least 28 KW
  • Tube Current Range: 10mA～233mA
    • kV Mode: 80 - 140 kV with at least 4 option

Detector

Slices/Rotation: At least 16 slice

  • Detector Rows: At least 32 Rows
  • Detector Coverage: At least 20mm

Computer Console

  • CPU: Host CPU, Dual Core; Recon CPU, Quad Core Intel Xeon or better
  • RAM storage: Host, 16G; Recon, 8G or more
  • Image Capacity: At least 500GB
  • Display Monitor: At least 23” Color LCD Monitor
  • Monitor Resolution: At least 1280 x 1024
  • Standard DICOM

Computer Workstation

  • CPU: 6 Core
  • RAM storage: 16G
  • Image Capacity: At least 500GB
  • Display Monitor: At least 19” Color LCD Monitor
  • Monitor Resolution: At least 1280 x 1024

Standard DICOM

  • Reconstruction Speed: At least 12 images/second
  • Multiple Reconstruction matrix: 512x512, 768x768,1024 x 1024 or better
  • Display Image matrix: 512x512, 768x768,1024 x 1024 or better.
  • Thinnest Slice: Not more than 0.625mm Number of View/Rotation: At least 2320 view
  • High-contrast Specification: >15lp/cm @ 0% MTF Low-contrast Specification: 0.3% as better as 4mm
  • Slow Pitch Scanning: At most 0.3 pitch
  • Fast Pitch Scanning: At least 1.5 pitch

Software

  • Real Time Topography : Must present
  • Automatic Monitoring of Contrast Enhancement: Must be present
  • Auto Start Scan when HU reaches threshold: Must be present
  • MIPS: Must be present
  • Multi-planar Reformation (MPR): Must be present
  • Pediatric Protocol: Must be present
  • Dose Modulation: Must be present
  • Dose Computation and Display: Must be present
  • Vessel Measurable Analysis
  • Virtual Endoscopy: Must be present
  • Automatic Bone Removal: Must be present
  • CT Angiography: Must be present

Others

  • Room Requirement of Gantry Room: Can fit in 19.0 Sq. m
  • Power Requirement: Not more than 50 kVA
  • Gantry Weight: Less than 1800 kg
